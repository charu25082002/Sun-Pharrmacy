About Us

Committed to Caring for Your Health
Since 1983

Sun Pharrmacy has been a trusted name in healthcare since 1983, serving generations with dedication, integrity, and compassion. Founded by K. Sivakumar, our pharmacy was built on a simple yet powerful mission — to make quality healthcare accessible, affordable, and reliable for everyone.

For over four decades, we have provided both prescription and over-the-counter medicines, ensuring every product meets the highest standards of safety and authenticity. We work closely with licensed pharmacists and healthcare professionals to deliver accurate guidance and dependable service you can trust.
